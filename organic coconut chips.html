Description

Nutrinut Organic Coconut Chips are pure, natural (raw) chips of organic coconut meat. Unlike the toasted version, these are simply dried without being roasted. This gives them a softer, chewier texture and a milder, creamy coconut flavor. They retain all their natural oils and nutrients.

Key Benefits

  • Source of Healthy Fats: Rich in natural coconut oils (MCTs) that your body can use for quick, sustained energy.
  • High in Dietary Fiber: Naturally high in fiber, which is essential for good digestion and helps you feel full.
  • Clean & Raw: Certified organic, meaning they are non-GMO and grown without synthetic pesticides. Being raw, they are unprocessed by heat.
  • Versatile Ingredient: The chewy texture is perfect for adding to trail mixes, granola, and baked goods, or for snacking on straight from the bag.
