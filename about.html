Our Story: Pure Goodness from the Heart of Nature

We are a memeber of Nawaloka Medicare and a producer and exporter of coconut kernel based value added products from Sri Lanka for more than 30 countries since 2016. Our strengthen quality aspects and sustainable production methods support us to deliver premium quality products at affordable prices to worldover prestigeous customers. Our own modern state-of-art production facility is in Kurunegala where we have easy access to source raw nuts directly from farmers eleminating intermediaries in supplychain. We are capable to handle both retail and bulk packaging and any private branding as per your buying needs.

We have encouraged social care, women partnership & profit flowing system up to the farmers. As a leader in the industry we assure our capabilities of financial stability, continuous supply, ontime delivery, transperency in business transactions, flexible to cater to your need, prompt in communication, competitive in pricing and assured quality with all the products.
