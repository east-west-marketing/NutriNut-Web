Description

At NutriNut, we produce natural soap using Organic Virgin Coconut Oil, blended with 100% natural Ayurvedic herbals, sustainable plant-based essential oils, and natural fragrances. Our soaps are completely free from harsh chemicals, pore-clogging animal fats, lathering agents, and synthetic preservatives.We utilize the traditional Cold Process method, hand-making every bar in small batches. This process ensures that all the goodness and rich nutrient profiles of the natural ingredients are secured in the final, long-lasting bar. Each bar is cured for 6-10 weeks to achieve a mild, rich lather.We ensure an Eco-friendly process to keep your skin happy and healthy while securing environmental sustainability.

Key Benefits

  • Ultra-Clean Ingredients: Made with Organic Virgin Coconut Oil and 100% natural herbals, free from harsh chemicals, synthetic preservatives, and pore-clogging animal fats.
  • Nutrient-Rich Formula: The Cold Process method is used in small batches to secure the goodness and rich nutrient profile of every ingredient.
  • Mild & Long-Lasting: Each bar is cured for 6-10 weeks, naturally hardening into a mild, rich-lathering, and long-lasting soap.
  • Natural Moisturization: The saponification process naturally creates Glycerol, a crucial component that keeps your skin happy and healthy by locking in moisture.
  • Eco-Conscious Production: We ensure an Eco-friendly process by preventing the use of harsh chemicals
